# 📊 Sales-profit-dashboard-Excel
Interactive Excel dashboard analyzing sales and profit trends using Pivot Tables, slicers, and data visualization for business insights.
🔍 Overview

This project focuses on analyzing sales and profit data to uncover business performance trends across categories, customers, and regions. The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor KPIs and make data-driven decisions effectively.

⚠️ Problem Statement

Businesses often struggle to understand:

Which product categories generate maximum profit
Monthly and yearly sales trends
High-value customers and regions
Performance gaps across categories

This results in delayed and inefficient decision-making.

🎯 Objective
Analyze sales and profit trends
Track category-wise and monthly performance
Identify top customers contributing to profit
Monitor regional (state-wise) sales distribution
Build an interactive dashboard for quick insights

📊 Dashboard Features

✅ Key Metrics (KPIs)
Total Sales

Total Profit

📈 Visual Insights
Sales by Category
Sales by Month
Customer Count by Year
Top 5 Customers by Profit
Sales by State (Geographical Analysis)

🎛️ Interactive Filters
Category
Year

💡 Key Insights

🔸 Certain categories contribute higher revenue but lower profit

🔸 Sales show seasonal trends with peak months

🔸 A small group of customers contributes major profit

🔸 Regional performance varies significantly across states
